#Basic Commands

## Paket-Manager [Pacman](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/pacman)

## Updaten

````
pacman -Syu
````

## Installieren

````
pacman -S package_name1 package_name2 ...
````

## Entfernen

### Entfernt Packet
````
pacman -R package_name
````
